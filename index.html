What is Responsive Design?

It is a web design and development technique that through the use of flexible structures (flexible containers, flexible images and video) and together with Media Queries specified in CSS, manage to adapt a website to the environment of the device in which it is located.
With this technique, the content of your site's design looks good and can be quite readable for users on the device with which it interacts.

On the other hand, to work correctly in responsive designs, we must bear in mind that we must work with relative units.
